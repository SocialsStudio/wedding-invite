Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Where should we stay?

+

There are many beautiful hotels and accommodations near the venue in Chiang Mai. We recommend booking as soon as possible as October is high season in Chiang Mai and places fill up quickly. Popular areas include the Old City, Riverside, and Nimman neighborhoods.

How do we get around Chiang Mai?

+

Chiang Mai has Grab (similar to Uber), which is the easiest and most affordable way to get around the city. Simply download the Grab app before your trip. Taxis and tuk-tuks are also available, but we recommend using Grab for convenience and transparent pricing.

Can I bring a plus one?

+

Each invitation allows for a maximum of 2 guests. Please let us know the name of your +1 when you RSVP so we can prepare accordingly.

What should we wear?

+

We kindly request our guests to wear beige or sage green to complement our wedding theme and the beautiful Chiang Mai setting. Think elegant garden party attire in these soft, natural tones.
