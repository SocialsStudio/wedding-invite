Accommodation

+

Chiang Mai offers a wide range of beautiful hotels and accommodations close to the venue. You can easily find options on Airbnb, Booking.com, and Agoda.

As October is high season, we recommend booking as early as possible, as rooms fill up quickly.

Popular areas to consider include the Night Bazaar, Old Town, Riverside, and Nimman neighborhoods. For convenience, we suggest staying within 15 minutes of the venue.

Transportation

+

Chiang Mai is easy to get around. We recommend using Grab (similar to Uber), which is convenient, affordable, and has transparent pricing—simply download the app before your trip.

Taxis and tuk-tuks are also widely available, and there is an airport taxi service that is easy to book upon arrival. You may also rent a car or motorbike if you have an international driving license.
